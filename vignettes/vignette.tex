\section{PRS-PGx-TL}\label{prs-pgx-tl}}

A transfer learning (TL) based method to leverage large-scale disease
GWAS summary statistics and individual-level pharmacogenomics (PGx) data
to predict drug response

We designed a total of 6 models of PRS-PGx-TL depending on the following
criteria:

\textbf{Updating Parameters}: - Whether to update \$ \beta\emph{G \$ and
\$ \beta}\{GT\} \$ at the same time, or fix \$ \beta\_G =
\beta\emph{G\^{}\{pre\} \$ and only update \$ \beta}\{GT\} \$.

**Initial Values for \$ \beta\_\{GT\} \$**: - Whether to set the initial
values for \$ \beta\_\{GT\} \$ as 0 or \$ \beta\_G\^{}\{pre\} \$.

\textbf{Criterion for Parameter Tuning}: - Whether to maximize the
overall \$ R\^{}2 \$ or the conditional \$ R\^{}2 \$ of \$ G \times T \$
(conditional on \$ G \$) as a criterion for parameter tuning.

The choice of M1-M6 corresponds to the following arguments:
